# Research Basis

Use this when a user asks why the skill recommends interactive signature features, or when a feature needs extra strategic justification.

## What The Research Implies

### Message Match Is Non-Negotiable

Google Ads guidance says visitors expect the landing page to match the ad they clicked, including CTA and offer. Google's 2025 ads-quality update also emphasizes relevant, easy-to-navigate landing pages.

Implication: the signature feature should fulfill the angle's promise within the first screen or first interaction. Do not bury the promised calculator, map, quiz, or guide under generic brand copy.

Sources:

- https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/6238826
- https://blog.google/products/ads-commerce/search-ads-and-the-importance-of-landing-page-navigation/

### Interactive Content Makes Value Felt

Content Marketing Institute cites analysis where interactive content produced materially more engagement and time spent than static content. It also highlights calculators as useful lead-gen tools when they return a personalized report or scenario.

Implication: prefer calculators, quizzes, maps, configurators, and diagnostic scans when the offer is abstract. The interaction must produce an immediate useful result, not just collect answers.

Source:

- https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/articles/interactive-content-drive-leads/

### Personalization Needs Transparency

Twilio's 2025 customer engagement research reports that many consumers abandon irrelevant experiences, while real-time personalization can increase buying intent. The same report warns that consumers want control and transparency around data and AI.

Implication: ask for zero-party data directly, explain why each input matters, and show a useful output immediately. For AI-flavored features, state limits and avoid black-box authority.

Source:

- https://www.twilio.com/en-us/press/releases/socer-2025

### Performance Is Part Of Conversion

Core Web Vitals now emphasize LCP, INP, and CLS. INP measures how responsive interactions feel, which matters for calculators, sliders, quizzes, dashboards, and embedded widgets.

Implication: signature features must be fast on mobile, use stable layout dimensions, avoid heavy above-the-fold scripts unless necessary, and be verified after implementation.

Source:

- https://web.dev/articles/defining-core-web-vitals-thresholds

### Finance Features Need Fair, Balanced Framing

The SEC marketing rule prohibits misleading implications and says potential benefits need fair and balanced treatment of risks and limitations. It also treats hypothetical performance carefully.

Implication: show scenarios, assumptions, risks, and limits. Avoid guaranteed return language, selective performance curves, or outputs that look like personalized regulated advice.

Source:

- https://www.sec.gov/resources-small-businesses/small-business-compliance-guides/investment-adviser-marketing

### AI Claims Must Be Substantiated

The SEC has brought actions against investment advisers for misleading statements about AI use, warning against "AI washing."

Implication: if a landing says GPT, AI, algorithmic, automated, or predictive, the feature should either demonstrate a real supported capability or use restrained copy such as "educational AI-style preview." Do not invent AI capabilities for conversion.

Source:

- https://www.sec.gov/newsroom/press-releases/2024-36

### Endorsements And Reviews Need Disclosure

FTC endorsement guidance says endorsements must be honest, not misleading, and material connections should be clearly disclosed.

Implication: do not fabricate testimonials, logos, ratings, review counts, or success stories. If using social proof, keep it truthful and disclose compensation or relationships when relevant.

Source:

- https://www.ftc.gov/business-guidance/resources/ftcs-endorsement-guides-what-people-are-asking

### Meta Lead Gen Rewards The Full Funnel

Meta's lead ads guidance emphasizes instant forms, website forms, CRM sync, conversion leads optimization, and AI/automation. Meta also presents messaging as a lead format where automated questions should start easy, qualify gradually, and set clear next steps.

Implication: a landing feature should collect useful qualification data in a way that can feed the lead flow. For high-consideration offers, introduce intentional friction through a diagnostic or review step instead of only chasing cheap form submits.

Sources:

- https://www.facebook.com/business/ads/ad-objectives/lead-generation/lead-ads-with-forms
- https://www.facebook.com/business/ads/ad-objectives/lead-generation/lead-ads-with-messaging
- https://www.facebook.com/business/ads/meta-advantage-plus/leads

### TradingView Adds Real Market Gravity

TradingView docs describe widgets as embeddable financial tools with built-in market data, available as iframe or Web Component formats.

Implication: use TradingView modules when live market context improves seriousness. Pair them with educational interpretation and disclaimers, not buy/sell language.

Source:

- https://www.tradingview.com/widget-docs/getting-started/
